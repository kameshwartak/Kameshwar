Shram Suikriti (Labour Approval) in Nepal

Shram Suikriti (Labour Approval) is a mandatory approval issued by the Government of Nepal for citizens who are going abroad for foreign employment. It verifies that a worker’s job offer, visa, contract, and employer details are legally approved under Nepal’s foreign employment laws. Without Shram Suikriti, a Nepali worker cannot legally depart from Tribhuvan International Airport for overseas employment.

The process protects workers from fraud, illegal recruitment, and exploitation by ensuring proper documentation, insurance coverage, welfare fund contribution, and government registration. Labour approval can now be applied online through the official Department of Foreign Employment (DoFE) portal, making the system faster, safer, and more transparent.
