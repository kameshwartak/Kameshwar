How to Apply for Shram Suikriti (Labour Approval) Online in Nepal – Complete 2026 Guide

Shram Suikriti (also known as Labour Approval) is a mandatory government approval issued by the Department of Foreign Employment (DoFE), Nepal. It verifies your employment contract, visa, salary, and employer before you travel abroad for work.

Without a valid Shram Suikriti, you cannot board a flight from Tribhuvan International Airport for foreign employment. The online process through the official FEIMS portal makes it faster and more transparent.

Official Portal for Shram Suikriti Application

Apply directly through the government’s official system:

https://feims.dofe.gov.np

Step-by-Step Process to Apply Shram Suikriti Online

  1. Visit the official FEIMS DoFE portal.
  2. Register or log in using your Nepali citizenship number.
  3. Fill the online Labour Approval application form accurately.
  4. Upload all required documents in clear scanned copies.
  5. Pay the government fee and Foreign Employment Welfare Fund contribution.
  6. Submit the application and track status in real-time.
  7. Download the approved Shram Suikriti certificate once approved.

Required Documents for Shram Suikriti 2026

  • Valid Nepali Passport (minimum 2 years validity)
  • Valid Work Visa / Work Permit of the destination country
  • Original Employment Contract (attested)
  • Foreign Employment Insurance Policy
  • Medical Fitness Certificate from DoFE-approved center
  • Pre-Departure Orientation Training (PDOT) Certificate
  • Employer Company Registration Documents (if required)

Government Fees & Welfare Fund

You are required to pay the prescribed government processing fee and contribute to the Foreign Employment Welfare Fund. This fund supports workers and families in case of distress, rescue, or compensation.

How to Renew Shram Suikriti

Renewal is also done online through the same FEIMS portal if you change employer, extend your contract, or return and go again.

Pro Tip: Always apply through the official government portal to avoid middlemen fraud and delays.

Frequently Asked Questions about Shram Suikriti

What is Shram Suikriti (Labour Approval) in Nepal?

Shram Suikriti is a mandatory government approval issued by the Department of Foreign Employment (DoFE) for Nepali citizens going abroad for foreign employment. It verifies your job offer, contract, visa, and employer details.

How can I apply for Shram Suikriti online?

You can apply online through the official FEIMS portal: https://feims.dofe.gov.np. Register, fill the form, upload documents, pay fees, and track your application.

What documents are required for Shram Suikriti?

Required documents include: Valid Nepali Passport, Work Visa, Employment Contract, Foreign Employment Insurance, Medical Fitness Certificate, and Pre-Departure Orientation Training Certificate.

How long does it take to get Shram Suikriti approval?

Processing time usually ranges from 3 to 15 working days depending on document verification.

Can I renew my Shram Suikriti online?

Yes, renewal can also be done online through the same FEIMS portal.
