How to Apply Shram Suikriti Online in Nepal

Shram Suikriti (Labour Approval) is a mandatory approval issued by the Government of Nepal for citizens travelling abroad for foreign employment. This approval confirms that the worker’s job offer, employment contract, visa, salary, and employer details are verified and legally registered under Nepal’s Foreign Employment Act.

Without valid Shram Suikriti, Nepali workers are not allowed to depart from Tribhuvan International Airport for overseas jobs. The approval process ensures worker safety, prevents illegal recruitment, and confirms that all employment documents follow government regulations. Today, workers can apply for labour approval online through the official Department of Foreign Employment (DoFE) portal.

Official Online Portal for Labour Approval

The Government of Nepal provides an official online system where workers can apply for labour approval or renew their existing approval. You can access the official portal here:

https://feims.dofe.gov.np

This portal allows Nepali workers to register, upload documents, track application status, and receive approval digitally without visiting multiple government offices.

Step-by-Step Process to Apply Shram Suikriti Online

Follow these steps to apply for Shram Suikriti online in Nepal:

Step 1: Visit the official Foreign Employment Information Management System (FEIMS) portal.
Step 2: Create a new account using your citizenship number and personal details.
Step 3: Fill out the online labour approval application form.
Step 4: Upload required documents such as passport copy, visa, employment contract, and insurance.
Step 5: Pay the required government fees and welfare fund contribution.
Step 6: Submit the application and track approval status online.
Step 7: Once approved, download or print your labour approval certificate.

Required Documents for Shram Suikriti

Applicants must prepare the following documents before applying:

• Valid Nepali Passport
• Valid Work Visa
• Employment Contract from Employer
• Insurance Policy for Foreign Employment
• Medical Report from Approved Medical Center
• Pre-Departure Orientation Training Certificate

Government Fees and Welfare Fund

Workers must contribute to the Foreign Employment Welfare Fund and pay government processing fees during the application. These funds help provide compensation, rescue support, and assistance to workers and their families in case of emergencies abroad.

Renewal of Labour Approval

If a worker changes employers or returns home and goes abroad again, they must renew their labour approval. The renewal process can also be completed online through the same FEIMS system.
